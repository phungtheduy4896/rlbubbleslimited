Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how R L BUBBLES LIMITED collects, uses, stores, and protects information relating to visitors, customers, and website enquiries. It is written with UK business use, transparency, and advertising platform review in mind.

What information we collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when contacting us or booking an appointment, such as your name, email address, phone number, preferred service, and enquiry details. We may also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referral source, and pages visited.

How we use your information

We use information to respond to enquiries, arrange appointments, manage customer communication, improve website performance, maintain security, meet legal obligations, and understand the effectiveness of our advertising and website content.

Legal basis for processing

Where applicable under UK data protection law, we rely on legitimate interests, contractual necessity, legal obligations, and consent where consent is required, including for certain analytics, advertising, and non-essential cookies.

Google Ads, analytics and advertising

Our website may use Google Ads, Google Analytics, conversion tracking, remarketing, or similar services to understand website traffic and advertising performance. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to measure visits, conversions, and ad relevance. Google and other third parties may show ads based on previous visits to this website or other websites.

You can manage advertising preferences through Google ad settings and your browser settings where available.

Data sharing

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, support hosting, email handling, analytics, advertising measurement, fraud prevention, or technical maintenance. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for enquiry handling, record keeping, business administration, legal compliance, and dispute resolution.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to request access to personal information, correction of inaccurate data, deletion, restriction, objection to certain processing, and withdrawal of consent where consent applies.

Contact for privacy matters

For privacy questions or requests relating to personal information, please contact us at director@rlbubbleslimited.com or write to R L BUBBLES LIMITED, 14 AMHERST AVENUE W13 8NQ LONDON.
