Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the website and how users can understand or manage them.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, improve performance, analyse visits, and measure advertising effectiveness.

How we use cookies

We may use strictly necessary cookies, performance and analytics cookies, functionality cookies, and advertising or measurement cookies where applicable. Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on the website.

Google Ads and analytics cookies

Where advertising or analytics tools are in use, cookies may help measure campaign performance, understand site visits, attribute conversions, limit repetitive ads, and support remarketing. These technologies may be provided by Google or similar platforms.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Some browsers also allow you to block certain categories of cookies. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect site functionality or measurement accuracy.

Consent

Where legally required, non-essential cookies should be used only after appropriate consent. Continued site use alone may not be sufficient where consent standards require a clearer affirmative choice, so a cookie banner or consent tool is recommended when deploying advertising or analytics tags.

Further information

If you have questions about cookie use on this website, please contact R L BUBBLES LIMITED at director@rlbubbleslimited.com.
